The Experience

The Art of Brewing

Great tea is a ritual, not a routine. Follow these steps to unlock the full depth of flavour in every cup.

01

Heat

Bring fresh spring water to 195°F (90°C). Never use boiling water — it bruises the delicate first flush leaves.

02

Measure

Use 2.5 grams of tea per 200ml of water. The perfect ratio for extracting optimal flavour without bitterness.

03

Steep

Let the tea steep for 3–4 minutes. Watch as the leaves unfurl and release their golden amber essence.

04

Savour

Pour into a pre-warmed cup. Take a moment. Inhale the aroma before your first sip. Let the Himalayas come to you.
